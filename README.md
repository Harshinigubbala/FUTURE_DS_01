# FUTURE_DS_01

## Task 1 - Business Sales Performance Analytics

### Objective

Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

### Dashboard Features

* Total Sales KPI
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Region Slicer

### Tool Used

* Power BI

### Outcome

Created an interactive sales dashboard to support business decision-making and performance analysis.
